Heart Diseases

Heart Disease Model: 85.25%

Heart diseases are a group of conditions that affect the heart. These include coronary artery disease, heart failure, and heart attacks, all of which can make the heart work less well and even threaten your life.

Diabetes

Model: 85.25%

Diabetes is a chronic metabolic disease that causes high blood sugar levels. This happens when the body doesn't make enough insulin or doesn't use insulin well, which can lead to a number of health problems.

Parkinson's Diseases

Model: 85.25%

Parkinson’s disease is a brain disorder that causes unintended or uncontrollable movements, such as shaking, stiffness, and difficulty with balance and coordination.

Pneumonia

Model: 85.25%

Pneumonia is an infection that affects one or both lungs. It causes the air sacs, or alveoli, of the lungs to fill up with fluid or pus. Bacteria, viruses, or fungi may cause pneumonia.
